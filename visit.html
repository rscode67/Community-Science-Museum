Accessibility

The museum includes wheelchair access, clear signage, sensory accommodations like captions and audio descriptions, digital accessibility through websites and apps, specialized programs, trained staff assistance, and pre-visit details to ensure a welcoming and inclusive experience for all visitors.

Food and Drinks

Located on the ground floor near the main entrance, the Museum Cafe offers a variety of delicious meals and snacks for all tastes. Open daily from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM, the cafe serves fresh salads, sandwiches, soups, and hot meals, with plenty of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options available. Enjoy a range of hot beverages, including specialty coffees, teas, and fresh juices. For allergy information or special dietary requests, please ask our friendly staff.
