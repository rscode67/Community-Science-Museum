Wolly Mammoth

Journey back to the Ice Age and discover the world of the incredible Woolly Mammoth, one of the most iconic creatures to ever roam the Earth! Join us for an immersive and educational event that takes you deep into the history of these magnificent giants. Learn about their adaptations, including their thick fur, massive tusks, and large size, which allowed them to survive the harsh, freezing climates of the Ice Age.

Through interactive exhibits, life-sized replicas, and engaging hands-on activities, you'll explore how these majestic mammals lived, what they ate, and how they interacted with their environment. Hear from expert paleontologists and historians who will share fascinating insights about the behavior and habitat of the Woolly Mammoth, as well as the theories behind their extinction.

In addition to our educational displays, enjoy virtual reality experiences that allow you to walk alongside these creatures in their natural habitat, offering a truly immersive look at their world. Discover how scientists have studied ancient mammoth remains and what these findings reveal about the past.

This event is perfect for families, history buffs, and anyone curious about prehistoric life. Don’t miss this exciting opportunity to connect with the ancient world and get up close to one of nature’s most awe-inspiring creatures—the Woolly Mammoth!
