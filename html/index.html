The Biology and Medicine Exhibition at the museum is an exciting adventure into the world of science, perfect for young explorers! With fun, interactive displays, kids can discover how our bodies work, how doctors help us stay healthy, and the amazing science behind medicine. From learning about the heart’s beats to exploring the tiny cells that make us who we are, the exhibition brings biology to life with hands-on activities, games, and cool models. Young visitors can also see how scientists use technology to find new ways to treat illnesses and improve our health, all while having fun and learning about the wonders of life and medicine.

An aircraft simulation for kids lets them feel like they’re flying a plane! They sit in a fun, realistic cockpit with buttons and levers, and watch a big screen showing the view from the sky. The seat moves to make it feel like they’re flying, and there are sounds like engine roars and radio chatter. It’s a cool way to learn about airplanes while having an exciting adventure!
